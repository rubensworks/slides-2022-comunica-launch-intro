The Comunica Association

  • A non-profit for the sustainability of Comunica

    Open-source JS/TS framework for querying Knowledge Graphs on the Web

  • Receives funding from members and sponsors

    Pays developers for bugs and features on the roadmap
    Members collectively determine the long-term roadmap

  • Management of bounties

    Companies place bounties on bugs/features, claimed by developers

Current Roadmap (2022)

  • Future-oriented development

    Become a testbed for new features and techniques.
    RDF-star, SPARQL 1.2, WebAssembly, ESM, ...

  • Improving overall performance

    Implementation of algorithmic and low-level querying optimizations.

  • Developer experience

    Enhancements to make development in and with Comunica easier.

  • Outreach

    Connect with other communities with overlapping interests. (e.g. RDF/JS, Rollup.js)
https://comunica.dev/roadmap/
